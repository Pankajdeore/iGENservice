Privacy Policy

iGEN ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website www.igenservice.com. This page tells you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of the site.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you submit our contact form, including your name, email address, and message content. We do not sell or rent this information to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and requests
  • To send you relevant communications about iGEN services (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and services

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. We use:

  • Google Maps: The contact and home page embed a Google Maps iframe. Google may set cookies when the map loads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Session cookies: Used to remember your cookie preference (stored locally in your browser).
  • Tawk.to live chat (when enabled): If our live chat widget is active, Tawk.to may set cookies. See Tawk.to's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by FormSubmit.co, which forwards form submissions to our email address. FormSubmit does not store your data beyond the time needed to deliver the email.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the EU or California, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at org@igenservice.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
